Forward Kinematics

Kinematic chains

 

  • A robot manipulator is composed of a set of links connected together by joints.
    • Simple: revolute or prismatic with one degree-of-freedom (DoF)
    • Complex: universal or spherical with multiple DoF
  • No loss of generality considering only single DoF joints as multiple DoF joints can always be thought of as a succession of single DoF joints with links of length zero in between.

 

  • The action of each joint can be described by a single real number:

    • the angle of rotation in the case of a revolute joint
    • the displacement in the case of a prismatic joint

  • A robot manipulator with nn joints will have n+1n+1 links, since each joint connects two links.

  • Number the joints from 11 to nn and the links from 00 to nn, starting from the base.

  • Joint ii, which is fixed w.r.t. link iâˆ’1i-1, connects link iâˆ’1i-1 to link ii.
